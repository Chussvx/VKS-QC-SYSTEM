# VKS Quality Control (QC) Inspector Role

## Role Overview
The **QC Inspector** is the primary guardian of service quality at VKS. This role combines real-time monitoring via the **QC Dashboard** with active field engagement using the **Inspector App**. The Inspector ensures that guards are performing their duties correctly, sites are secure, and incidents are resolved swiftly.

---

## üõë Core Responsibilities

### 1. Real-Time Monitoring (The Watchtower)
The QC Inspector is responsible for actively monitoring the status of all deployed guards and patrols.
*   **Live Patrol Status**: Monitor the *Patrol Status* page to identify late patrols, missed checkpoints, or offline guards.
*   **Guard Attendance**: verify that guards have checked in on time via the *Dashboard* overview.
*   **Alert Response**: React immediately to "Late" or "SOS" system alerts.

### 2. Field Inspections (The Boots on the Ground)
Using the **Inspector App**, the QC Inspector visits sites to perform verifying checks.
*   **Uniform & Grooming**: Check that guards are properly uniformed and equipped (flashlight, baton, etc.).
*   **Post Standing**: Ensure guards are alert, not sleeping, and at their designated posts.
*   **Site Safety Checks**: Verify that site perimeter, locks, and lighting are adequate.
*   **Knowledge Check**: Quiz guards on site-specific protocols (e.g., "What do you do if a fire alarm rings?").

### 3. Incident Management & Support
The QC Inspector is the first responder (digitally or physically) to issues.
*   **Incident Logging**: Record theft, damage, or fights in the *Incidents* module.
*   **Complaint Resolution**: Investigate client complaints (e.g., "Guard was rude") and document findings.
*   **Disciplinary Action**: Issue "Yellow Cards" or warnings to guards found violating protocols during inspections.

### 4. Reporting & Guidance
*   **Shift Handover**: Review digital handover logs to ensure information is passed correctly between shifts.
*   **Coaching**: Provide on-site correction and training to guards who are underperforming.
*   **Performance Grading**: Contribute to the "Guard Fairness Score" by logging inspection results (Pass/Fail).

## üìã Daily User Journey (Task List)

### Start of Shift (Office/Remote)
- [ ] **Log in to QC Dashboard**: Check the "Live Operations" map.
- [ ] **Review Handover**: Read the previous shift's *Handover Log* for ongoing issues.
- [ ] **Check Attendance**: Call/Message any sites showing "No Show" status.

### Mid-Shift (Patrol & Monitoring)
- [ ] **Route Planning**: Select 3-5 sites to visit based on recent alerts or "At Risk" status (low performance score).
- [ ] **Conduct Inspections**:
    - Arrive at site.
    - Open **Inspector App**.
    - Scan Site QR.
    - Complete "Guard Readiness Checklist" (Uniform, Alertness, Equipment).
    - Rate the site condition (Cleanliness, Safety).
    - Submit report.
- [ ] **Monitor Patrols**: Between site visits, check the *Patrol Status* page on mobile/tablet. Call sites that are >15 mins late on patrols.

### End of Shift
- [ ] **Incident Summary**: Ensure all active incidents from the shift have a status update.
- [ ] **Digital Handover**: Submit the *Shift Handover Reporting* form, noting key issues for the next QC.
